# Research Note

## Working study title

Measuring stance and ambiguity in GLES open-ended political responses

## Short motivation

Open-ended political survey responses often contain hedging, indirect positioning, partial commitment, and context-sensitive meaning. This makes them a useful first test case for OpenCodebook as an interpretive coding workflow engine rather than only a demo script.

## Near-term study goals

- inspect the available open-ended response material
- define a first pilot sample
- refine the stance and ambiguity codebook
- prepare future reliability and disagreement analysis
